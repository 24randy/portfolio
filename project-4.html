Project

Project Neomarche



  • Space Design
  • blender
  • Unity
  • 3D

Involvement :

Date : September 2022

The goal of this project was to create a Fashion Runway set for the company 19G. The brand's idea presented to us included many interloping textures and styles that served as the major method of expression for them on top of that, all the products were digital. Keeping this in mind, I designed the runway set to provide a sharp contrast to the futuristic and shiny aesthetics they had. With earthy and rocky textures, the set was made to resemble an old dungeon with a modern touch to it, which was added in the shape of abstract glyphs on the walls and a ceiling waterfall that dropped straight down into the centre of the room.

The space includes a waterfall at the center which flows into a pool that is surround by a square walkway that runs across the walls of the room. In the middle of the pool, four platforms are raised on which 19G's digital clothing items shall be displayed. Since these items are digital, they shall be floating on top of the platform as it rotates so that the meta-users visiting this fashion-space can see every angle of the clothing as they walk around the room. Moreover, beside the product a rectangular screen will be levitating which will provide all details of that particular product.
