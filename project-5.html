Packaging Poster



  • Graphic Design
  • Branding
  • Illustrator
  • Photoshop

Involvement :

Date : July 2022

I had made this poster as a part of a branding project for a brand called "Nuty" which makes almond milk. My main though process and goal for this project was to design the packaging so that it catches a customer's eyes and also play with the typography in a way that it resembles a nut, making it more appealing and helping it garner more attention. The colours used are meant to make the branding stand out more so that it is clear without having to read its ingredients what the product actually is.
