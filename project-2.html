Project

Ubisoft Dashboard Design



  • UI/UX
  • Web
  • Figma
  • Photoshop

Involvement :

Date : October 2022

This website homepage was thoughtfully created keeping Assassins Creed Fans in mind, it is meant to be an extension to Ubisoft's platform. I wanted it to have an intriguing interface that captivated a gamer's attention, drawing their gaze towards it repeatedly. Moreover, I also wanted these gamers to have a smooth shopping experience just like how aspect of each game in the series has been carefully thought out to help players navigate it. The website will also include stills from the games's breathtaking graphics and world build.

The website also includes options for a gamer to be Abe to select their choice of game edition while also offering comparisons of different versions and what they include. Not only does this website aim to satisfy previous customers but also those who are new to the gaming industry. It makes the choice to enter the Assassin's Creed world just as thrilling as the games themselves.
